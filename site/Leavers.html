Leavers Process

Leavers can be submitted through the self-service portal.

Leaver’s requests can be raised through the self-service portal or manually via SCSM as a Service Request.
A leaver request will usually come in through the self-service portal or via email.

How to raise a Leaver Request via Self Service Portal?

  1. Go to myit.woodgroup.com
  2. Click on new start, transfer or leaver
  3. Click on go to request form
  4. Now continue inputting user details into the form. Don't leave any sections blank.

Points to consider:

Once you have completed all 7 pages of the form you can submit the request. What happens now is:

  1. The ticket will create an automatic MA which will be sent for approval.
  2. The ticket will automatically be assigned to the Service Desk queue
    where the queue manager will keep the ticket until the approval is received.
  3. Once the approval is received, the ticket will be assigned to the relevant teams to process.

How to manually raise a Leaver Request?

These requests will normally come in via email because it’s too much information to enter into a request over the phone.

Note – depending on the type of user access they require you will have to log several tickets.
For example, a new joiner account who requires an email ID, a phone ID and access to a shared mailbox would require 4 separate tickets.

To proceed with the request, you need to gather the minimum data. Once you have received a request you need to confirm you have the following information:

	Title
	Office (Where the employee will be reporting to)
	Office phone
	Employee’s manager
	Full company name
	Network access to be removed (e.g. shared drives/server access/mailbox access)
	Did you have a telephone extension which will be deleted? (telephony number for office phone)
	Do you currently have a smart phone?

If you haven’t been provided with the correct information then you can email the requester.
